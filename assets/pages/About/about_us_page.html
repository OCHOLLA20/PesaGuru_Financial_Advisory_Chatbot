About PesaGuru

Revolutionizing financial advisory services for Kenyans

Our Mission

PesaGuru is an AI-enabled chatbot designed to provide tailored financial advice and investment planning resources specifically for Kenyan users. Our mission is to democratize financial advice, making it accessible to low and middle-income consumers who traditionally could not access such services.

In the face of almost completely unavailable financial planning, rapid digitization offers a once-in-a-lifetime chance to democratize financial advice using technology. PesaGuru bridges this gap by utilizing artificial intelligence to offer personalized financial guidance that adapts to each user's unique needs and goals.

Whether you're a young professional looking to start investing, a student managing your finances, or an experienced professional planning for retirement, PesaGuru delivers relevant, actionable financial advice to help you achieve your financial goals.

Our Vision for the Future

Where we see PesaGuru in the years to come

Transforming Financial Literacy in Kenya

PesaGuru aims to become the go-to platform for financial advice and literacy in Kenya, empowering millions of Kenyans to make informed financial decisions. Our vision extends beyond just providing advice; we want to create a culture of financial awareness and planning across all socioeconomic segments.

As we grow, we plan to expand our capabilities to include more sophisticated financial planning tools, deeper integration with financial institutions, and enhanced personalization through advanced machine learning techniques.

By democratizing access to quality financial advice, we believe we can contribute to greater financial inclusion and economic prosperity for all Kenyans.
