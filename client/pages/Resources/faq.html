Getting Started with PesaGuru

How do I create a PesaGuru account?

Creating a PesaGuru account is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  1. Download the PesaGuru app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store, or visit our website at www.pesaguru.co.ke
  2. Click on "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
  3. Enter your phone number and email address
  4. Create a strong password
  5. Verify your phone number with the OTP (One-Time Password) sent to you
  6. Complete your profile by providing your personal details
  7. Accept the terms and conditions

Once these steps are completed, you'll have access to basic features. To unlock full financial advisory services, you'll need to complete account verification.

What documents do I need to verify my account?

To fully verify your PesaGuru account and access all features, you'll need to provide:

  • Identification documents: A clear photo of your National ID card, Passport, or Alien ID (both sides)
  • Proof of residence: A utility bill (water, electricity, internet) not older than 3 months, or a bank statement
  • A selfie: A clear photo of yourself holding your ID document

These documents are required to comply with Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations set by the Central Bank of Kenya and Capital Markets Authority.

How do I open a CDS account for NSE trading?

A Central Depository System (CDS) account is required to trade on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE). To open one through PesaGuru:

  1. Ensure your PesaGuru account is fully verified
  2. Navigate to "Investment" > "Stock Market" > "Open CDS Account"
  3. Fill in the required information and submit the digital CDS form
  4. Upload your KYC documents if prompted
  5. Sign the digital forms using your PIN

PesaGuru partners with licensed stockbrokers in Kenya to facilitate your CDS account opening. The process typically takes 2-3 business days. Once approved, your CDS account number will be displayed in your profile, and you can start trading NSE stocks.

Is PesaGuru available outside Kenya?

Currently, PesaGuru is optimized for the Kenyan market and available primarily to Kenyan residents or citizens. However, our services can be accessed globally with some limitations:

  • Kenyan citizens abroad: Can use PesaGuru for investment planning and monitoring their Kenyan investments
  • Non-Kenyan residents: Can access market insights and educational resources, but may face restrictions for trading and certain payment services

To use PesaGuru outside Kenya, you'll need:

  • A Kenyan phone number for verification
  • A Kenyan bank account or M-Pesa account for transactions

We're working on expanding our services to other East African countries in the near future.

What are the risks involved in trading on NSE?

Investing in the Nairobi Securities Exchange, like any stock market, involves various risks that you should be aware of:

  • Market risk: Stock prices can fluctuate due to overall market movements, impacting your investment value
  • Liquidity risk: Some NSE stocks have low trading volumes, making it difficult to buy or sell quickly without affecting the price
  • Company-specific risk: Individual companies face business risks that can affect their stock performance
  • Economic risk: Kenya's economic conditions, including inflation, interest rates, and currency fluctuations, can impact stock performance
  • Political risk: Political events and policy changes can create market volatility
  • Sector risk: Certain industries may underperform due to sector-specific challenges

To mitigate these risks:

  • Diversify your investments across different companies and sectors
  • Consider your investment time horizon (longer periods typically reduce volatility risk)
  • Only invest money you can afford to lose
  • Stay informed about market developments
  • Use PesaGuru's risk assessment tools to match investments to your risk tolerance

Remember: Past performance is not indicative of future results. All investments carry risk, and it's important to make informed decisions based on your personal financial situation and goals.

Deposits, Withdrawals & Payments

How do I deposit money into my PesaGuru account?

PesaGuru offers several convenient methods to deposit funds into your account:

  1. M-Pesa:
    • Go to the "Deposit" section in the app
    • Select "M-Pesa" as your deposit method
    • Enter the amount you wish to deposit
    • Confirm the transaction and you'll receive an M-Pesa prompt on your phone
    • Enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete the transaction
  2. Bank Transfer:
    • Use the unique bank account details provided in your PesaGuru account
    • Make a transfer from your bank
    • Funds typically reflect within 1-2 business days
  3. Card Payment:
    • Select "Card" as your deposit method
    • Enter your card details (we accept Visa, Mastercard, and most major cards)
    • Complete the secure payment process

Deposits via M-Pesa are processed instantly, while bank transfers may take 1-2 business days to reflect in your account. The minimum deposit amount is KSh 100, and there is no maximum limit for verified accounts.

Does PesaGuru support M-Pesa transactions?

Yes, PesaGuru fully integrates with M-Pesa, Kenya's leading mobile money service, offering comprehensive support for various transactions:

  • Deposits: Instantly fund your PesaGuru account using M-Pesa
  • Withdrawals: Transfer funds from PesaGuru to your M-Pesa account
  • Investments: Make direct investments using your M-Pesa balance
  • Bill payments: Pay bills directly through the app
  • Scheduled transactions: Set up recurring deposits or payments

To use M-Pesa with PesaGuru:

  1. Ensure your PesaGuru account is registered with the same phone number as your M-Pesa account
  2. Verify your phone number during the account setup process
  3. Navigate to "Payments" or "Deposit" within the app and select the M-Pesa option

Our M-Pesa integration is secured through the official Safaricom Daraja API, ensuring all transactions are safe and compliant with regulatory requirements.

Are there any transaction fees?

PesaGuru maintains a transparent fee structure for various transactions:

Deposit Fees:

  • M-Pesa: Standard Safaricom charges apply (typically 0-0.5% of transaction value)
  • Bank transfers: No charge from PesaGuru, but your bank may apply their standard EFT fees
  • Card payments: 2.5% of deposit amount

Withdrawal Fees:

  • To M-Pesa: KSh 20 for withdrawals below KSh 5,000; KSh 35 for withdrawals above KSh 5,000
  • To bank account: KSh 50 flat fee

Investment Transaction Fees:

  • NSE stocks: 1.5% of transaction value (includes broker commission and statutory charges)
  • Treasury bonds/bills: 0.2% of transaction value
  • Money market funds: No direct fees (fund managers charge management fees from returns)

Account Maintenance:

  • Basic account: No monthly fees
  • Premium account: KSh 250 per month (includes advanced financial advisory and premium features)

All fees are transparently displayed before you confirm any transaction. PesaGuru does not charge any hidden fees.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times vary depending on the withdrawal method:

  • M-Pesa withdrawals: Processed instantly, funds typically reflect in your M-Pesa account within minutes
  • Bank account withdrawals:
    • Same bank: 1 business day
    • Different bank: 1-3 business days

Withdrawal processing hours:

  • M-Pesa withdrawals are processed 24/7
  • Bank withdrawals initiated after 3:00 PM or during weekends/public holidays will be processed on the next business day

Withdrawal limits:

  • Minimum withdrawal: KSh 100
  • Maximum daily withdrawal to M-Pesa: KSh 150,000 (per Safaricom limits)
  • Maximum daily withdrawal to bank account: KSh 1,000,000

For security reasons, first-time withdrawals to a new bank account may require additional verification and could take up to 24 hours to process.

Security & Privacy

How does PesaGuru protect my personal and financial data?

PesaGuru employs multiple layers of security to protect your personal and financial information:

  • End-to-end encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using AES-256 standards
  • Secure data storage: Personal and financial data is stored in encrypted databases with strict access controls
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA): Additional security layer beyond passwords
  • Biometric authentication: Support for fingerprint and facial recognition
  • Session timeouts: Automatic logout after periods of inactivity
  • Regular security audits: Our systems undergo regular penetration testing by cybersecurity experts
  • Compliance with regulations: We adhere to Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019) and international security standards

PesaGuru never stores your complete card details or M-Pesa PIN. We use tokenization for payment information, working with PCI-DSS compliant payment processors.

Our privacy policy (available in the app settings) details how we collect, use, and protect your data. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent, except when required by law.

What should I do if I suspect fraud?

If you suspect any fraudulent activity on your PesaGuru account, take these immediate steps:

  1. Secure your account:
    • Change your password immediately
    • Enable multi-factor authentication if not already active
    • Log out of all devices (option available in security settings)
  2. Report to PesaGuru:
    • Call our fraud hotline: 0800-PESAGURU (0800-72724878)
    • Use the "Report Fraud" option in the app menu
    • Email us at security@pesaguru.co.ke with details of the suspicious activity
  3. Document everything:
    • Take screenshots of any suspicious transactions or activities
    • Note down times and details of any unauthorized access

Our security team is available 24/7 and will:

  • Investigate the reported incident
  • Freeze your account temporarily if necessary to prevent further unauthorized activity
  • Guide you through the recovery process
  • Help you report to relevant authorities if needed

Remember: PesaGuru will never call or email you asking for your password, PIN, or full card details. Be vigilant against phishing attempts.

How can I change my password?

Changing your password in PesaGuru is a straightforward process:

Through the app or website:

  1. Log in to your PesaGuru account
  2. Navigate to "Settings" (gear icon) or "Profile"
  3. Select "Security" or "Account Security"
  4. Tap on "Change Password"
  5. Enter your current password
  6. Enter your new password (must meet our security requirements)
  7. Confirm your new password
  8. Tap "Save" or "Update"

You'll receive a confirmation email and/or SMS that your password has been changed.

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter your registered email address or phone number
  3. Select whether you want to receive a reset link via email or SMS
  4. Click on the reset link received
  5. Create a new password and confirm it

Password requirements:

  • Minimum 8 characters
  • At least one uppercase letter
  • At least one lowercase letter
  • At least one number
  • At least one special character (e.g., !, @, #, $)

For security purposes, we recommend changing your password regularly and avoiding using the same password across multiple services.
