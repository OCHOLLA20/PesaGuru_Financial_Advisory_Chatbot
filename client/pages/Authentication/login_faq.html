Frequently Asked Questions - Login & Account

Find answers to common questions about logging in, creating an account, and managing your PesaGuru profile.

Account Creation & Registration

How do I create a PesaGuru account?

Creating a PesaGuru account is quick and easy:

  1. Visit the PesaGuru homepage and click on "Sign Up" or "Register"
  2. Enter your email address, phone number, and create a strong password
  3. Complete the verification process by confirming your email or phone number
  4. Fill in your basic profile information to help us personalize your financial advice
  5. Review and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

Once completed, you'll have immediate access to your personalized financial advisory chatbot!

What information do I need to provide during registration?

During registration, you'll need to provide:

  • A valid email address
  • Your mobile phone number (for account verification and security)
  • A secure password
  • Basic personal information (name, age range)

After the initial registration, you can enhance your experience by answering questions about your financial goals, risk tolerance, and investment preferences. This helps PesaGuru provide more personalized financial advice.

Is my data secure during registration?

Yes, we take your data security very seriously. All information provided during registration is:

  • Encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Stored securely in compliance with Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019)
  • Never shared with unauthorized third parties
  • Protected by our comprehensive security measures

PesaGuru uses bank-level security protocols to ensure your personal and financial information remains private and secure.

Is there a fee to create an account?

No, creating a basic PesaGuru account is completely free. We offer:

  • Free account creation and setup
  • Free access to basic financial advisory services
  • Free financial education resources

We do offer premium subscription plans with advanced features, but you can use the core features of PesaGuru without any cost.

Login Issues & Troubleshooting

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps to reset it:

  1. On the login page, click "Forgot Password" or "Reset Password"
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link (also check your spam/junk folder)
  4. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password
  5. Use your new password to log in

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 30 minutes. If your link has expired, simply restart the process.

I'm getting an "Invalid Login Credentials" error. What should I do?

If you're seeing an "Invalid Login Credentials" error, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Double-check that you're using the correct email address
  2. Ensure your password is entered correctly (check caps lock)
  3. Try the "Forgot Password" option to reset your password
  4. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again
  5. Try using a different web browser or device

If you still can't log in after trying these steps, please contact our support team for assistance.

I'm locked out of my account. What happened?

Your account may be temporarily locked for security reasons if:

  • There were multiple failed login attempts
  • Suspicious activity was detected on your account
  • You're trying to log in from an unrecognized device or location

To unlock your account:

  1. Wait 30 minutes and try logging in again
  2. Use the "Forgot Password" option to reset your password
  3. Check your email for any security notifications from PesaGuru
  4. Contact our support team if the issue persists
Can I log in using my phone number instead of email?

Yes, PesaGuru supports logging in with either your registered email address or phone number.

To log in with your phone number:

  1. On the login screen, select the "Phone Number" option
  2. Enter your registered phone number (starting with country code, e.g., +254)
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Log In"

Make sure the phone number you use is the same one you verified during registration.

Account Security & Management

How can I change my password?

To change your password:

  1. Log in to your PesaGuru account
  2. Go to "My Profile" or "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Security" or "Change Password"
  4. Enter your current password
  5. Enter and confirm your new password
  6. Click "Save" or "Update Password"

For security reasons, choose a strong password that includes a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.

How do I update my contact information?

To update your email address, phone number, or other contact information:

  1. Log in to your PesaGuru account
  2. Go to "My Profile" or "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Contact Information" or "Personal Details"
  4. Update your information as needed
  5. Verify your new email or phone number if prompted
  6. Click "Save" or "Update"

Keeping your contact information up-to-date ensures you receive important notifications about your account and financial advice.

Can I enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for my account?

Yes, we strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) for enhanced account security. To set up 2FA:

  1. Log in to your PesaGuru account
  2. Go to "My Profile" or "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Security" or "Two-Factor Authentication"
  4. Choose your preferred 2FA method (SMS or authenticator app)
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup

Once enabled, you'll need both your password and a verification code to log in, providing an extra layer of security for your account.

How do I delete my account?

If you wish to delete your PesaGuru account:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "My Profile" or "Account Settings"
  3. Scroll to the bottom and select "Delete Account"
  4. Read the information about account deletion
  5. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  6. Complete any final verification steps

Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal data and financial profiles will be removed in accordance with our data retention policy.

If you're experiencing issues with our service, we encourage you to contact our support team before deleting your account. We might be able to address your concerns.

Mobile & Multi-Device Access

Can I use PesaGuru on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access your PesaGuru account on multiple devices, including:

  • Smartphones (Android and iOS)
  • Tablets
  • Desktop computers
  • Laptops

Your account information, financial profiles, and chatbot conversation history will sync across all your devices automatically.

For security reasons, you may receive a notification when your account is accessed from a new device or location.

Is there a mobile app for PesaGuru?

Yes, PesaGuru has dedicated mobile apps for both Android and iOS devices:

  • Android users can download the app from Google Play Store
  • iOS users can download from the Apple App Store

The mobile app offers all the features of the web version, plus additional benefits:

  • Push notifications for important financial updates
  • Biometric login (fingerprint or face recognition)
  • Offline mode for basic financial guidance
  • USSD access for feature phone users

You can use the same login credentials for both the mobile app and the web version.

What should I do if I'm unable to log in on the mobile app?

If you're having trouble logging in on the mobile app, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  2. Check that you're using the correct email/phone and password
  3. Update the app to the latest version
  4. Clear the app cache (in your device settings)
  5. Restart your device and try again
  6. Uninstall and reinstall the app

If you still can't log in, try accessing your account through the web version to confirm your credentials are working correctly. If the issue persists, contact our support team.
