Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how PesaGuru collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of our financial advisory chatbot service. This policy applies to the PesaGuru application and all products and services offered by PesaGuru.

Last Updated: March 16, 2025

1. Information We Collect

PesaGuru collects several types of information from and about users of our service, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, age, gender, occupation, and other demographic information you provide during registration or when creating your financial profile.
  • Financial Information: Income details, savings, investments, financial goals, risk tolerance, spending habits, and financial preferences you share when using our service.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our chatbot, including queries, responses, feature usage, session duration, and frequency of use.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access PesaGuru, including device type, operating system, mobile network information, and IP address.
  • Location Information: General location information derived from your IP address or more precise GPS-based location if you grant permission.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Direct Collection: Information you provide during registration, onboarding, or through conversations with our chatbot.
  • Automated Collection: Using cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect usage information.
  • Third-Party Sources: With your consent, we may collect information from linked financial accounts, credit bureaus, and other public or private databases to enhance our service.
  • API Integrations: Information collected through integrations with financial institutions, market data providers, and payment processors (such as M-Pesa, bank APIs, NSE data).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized financial advice and investment planning recommendations.
  • Create and manage your user profile and customize your experience.
  • Analyze your financial situation, goals, and risk tolerance.
  • Process transactions and manage integrations with financial service providers.
  • Improve our chatbot's accuracy, responsiveness, and financial advice capabilities.
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates to our services, and promotional offers.
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and service features.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities, security breaches, and potential abuse of our service.
  • Comply with legal obligations, including regulations by the Capital Markets Authority (CMA) and Central Bank of Kenya (CBK).

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information:

  • Encryption: We use end-to-end encryption (AES-256) for all sensitive data storage and transmission.
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication for access to your account and sensitive financial functions.
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control to limit internal access to user data.
  • Regular Security Audits: Conducting periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosting on secure cloud services with industry-standard protections.
  • Data Minimization: Collecting only necessary information and anonymizing data where possible.

While we implement these safeguards, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to protect your information using current best practices.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in providing the PesaGuru service (cloud hosting, data analytics, customer support).
  • Financial Partners: Banks, investment platforms, and financial institutions to facilitate transactions or services you request.
  • Regulatory Authorities: When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, particularly to comply with CBK, CMA, and other Kenyan financial regulators.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, with appropriate data protection agreements in place.

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates.

6. Your Data Rights

Under Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data we hold.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Restrict Processing: Request restriction of processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Request the transfer of your personal data to another service.
  • Object: Object to processing of your personal data for certain purposes.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. We will also retain and use your information as necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Financial transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with Kenyan financial regulations. You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal requirements for data retention.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

PesaGuru uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

We use these technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Understand how you interact with our service.
  • Monitor aggregate usage and web traffic routing.
  • Improve our service and provide customized experiences.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.

9. Children's Privacy

PesaGuru does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information from our systems.

10. Third-Party Links and Services

PesaGuru may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by us. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

11. Compliance with Kenya's Data Protection Act

PesaGuru is committed to complying with Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019). This includes:

  • Processing personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
  • Collecting data for legitimate purposes and not further processing it incompatibly with those purposes.
  • Ensuring data is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary.
  • Maintaining accurate and up-to-date data.
  • Storing data securely and for no longer than necessary.
  • Implementing appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure data security.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

PesaGuru has the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the updated date at the top of this page. We encourage users to frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect.

13. Your Acceptance of These Terms

By using PesaGuru, you signify your acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our service. Your continued use of the service following the posting of changes to this policy will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.
