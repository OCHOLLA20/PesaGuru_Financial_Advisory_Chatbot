  • How does PesaGuru provide financial advice?
    PesaGuru uses AI technology to analyze your financial data and market trends to provide personalized financial advice. Our system takes into account your financial goals, risk tolerance, and current financial situation to recommend investment strategies that are tailored to your needs.
  • Is the financial advice from PesaGuru regulated?
    Yes, PesaGuru operates in compliance with financial regulations. However, it's important to note that while our AI provides suggestions based on data analysis, major financial decisions should always be made in consultation with a certified financial advisor.
  • How frequently is market data updated?
    PesaGuru updates market data in real-time during market hours. For stocks, bonds, and forex markets, data is refreshed every minute. For less liquid markets, updates occur at least every 15 minutes.
  • Can I customize which market data I see?
    Yes, you can customize your market data dashboard. Go to "Settings" > "Market Preferences" to select which markets, indices, or individual securities you want to track.
